ABOUT THE FOOTPRINT TRANSLATOR

Without context, the raw data from environmental impact analyses often mean little to consumers who want to better understand or compare products and services. We developed the Footprint Translator as a tool to help forward-thinking companies provide that context and improve they way they communicate, ultimately helping both the companies and their customers be better stewards of the environment.

Our combined expertise creates a rare mix of strong environmental science understanding coupled with savvy communication problem-solving. We love what we do, and would love to help you communicate your environmental data. Have a project in mind or just want to connect? Contact us!
